In the continent of Laeshyn, the land of Kragnahr has taken over large sums of the Western continent. With Kragnahr ruling the country with iron fist and adding policies like no half-bloods, resistance is sure to rise. Resistances come in different shapes and forms. Terrorism, diplomatic politics, saving those in need and anything in between. This is where you come in. You will be forced to make choices about how to most effectively deal with this. So how will YOU do it?
You used to be an ordinary animal... you think... And you had to deal with the balance of the jungle and life and theath scenarios on a daily basis. Then, one day you saw the light. Not only you, but every creature in the animal kingdom did. Now everyone has mutated into what looks like humans crossed with animals, and everyone is suddenly intelligent! You decide to band up with a party to learn more about this light. Maybe there resides even more power in there than originally perceived?
